SoulSmith: Forge your Destiny

Take control of a storm ravaged universe with your friends as a gods. Create your own creatures to fight, spells to conjure, and terrain to do battle upon.

Soulsmith is a game where you decide how a card gets to be played. Cards can be attacks, magic, flashes, terrain, or fixures. Players draft cards from the pack then create effects for their cards. But be warned, all cards are shuffled together before play, and your own secret weapon could be used against you.
